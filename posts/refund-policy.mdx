# Refund Policy

## Overview

SexyVoice.ai is committed to customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are provided for both one-time Top-up purchases and Subscription plans.

## Refund Eligibility

### Unused Credits

We offer refunds for unused credits within **90 days** of purchase. This applies to:

- **Top-up Purchases**: One-time credit purchases
- **Subscription Plans**: Monthly subscription charges

### Refund Conditions

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

1. The refund request must be submitted within 90 days of the original purchase date
2. At least 10% of your purchased credits must remain unused to be eligible for a refund (we do not process refunds for small remaining balances)
3. The request must come from the original account holder
4. The account must be in good standing with no violations of our Terms of Service

## Refund Process

1. **Submit a Request**: Contact our support team at [info@sexyvoice.ai](mailto:info@sexyvoice.ai) with your request or via the Support Chat in the [https://sexyvoice.ai/en/dashboard](https://sexyvoice.ai/en/dashboard)
2. **Verification**: We will verify your purchase and credit usage
3. **Approval**: Once approved, refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days
4. **Processing**: Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Dashboard Credits page: [https://sexyvoice.ai/en/dashboard/credits](https://sexyvoice.ai/en/dashboard/credits)

Once your subscription is cancelled, you will not be charged for future billing cycles, but no refunds will be issued for the current billing period unless within the 90-day window.

## Non-Refundable Situations

The following circumstances are **not eligible** for refunds:

- Credits that have been used or consumed
- Free credits
- Refund requests submitted after 90 days from purchase
- Chargebacks or disputes filed with your bank or credit card company
- Accounts violating our Terms of Service

## Payment Processor

Our payment processing is handled by Stripe. For additional payment-related inquiries, please review Stripe's terms and conditions at [stripe.com](https://stripe.com)

## Dispute Resolution

If you have a dispute regarding a refund:

1. First, contact our support team: [info@sexyvoice.ai](mailto:info@sexyvoice.ai)
2. We will work with you to resolve the issue within 14 days
3. If unresolved, you may file a dispute through your payment method's provider

## Contact Us

For refund inquiries or questions about this policy, please contact us at:

- **Email**: [info@sexyvoice.ai](mailto:info@sexyvoice.ai)
- **Support Chat**: [SexyVoice.ai Dashboard](https://sexyvoice.ai/en/dashboard)

## Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. Your continued use of SexyVoice.ai constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
